Images 2

Create an HTML file named images-2.html and a folder named images. Store the specified images in the folder:
  • Your baby picture
  • Your high school picture
  • Your recent picture
  • An picture of what you want to become in the future
In your HTML file, put the following information:
  • A title of you web page
  • A paragraph about your childhood life
  • A paragraph about your high school life
  • A paragraph about your current life
  • A paragraph about your future life
Insert the images in every paragraph in your web page. Resize the images if necessary. Link the images to the specified location:
  • Baby picture - W3Schools
  • High School Picture - text-formatting-1.html
  • Recent Picture - images-1.html
  • Future Self Picture - Google
